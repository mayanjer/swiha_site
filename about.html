About Swiha Tours & Travel

Ugandan at heart. World-class in service.

Our Story

Twelve Years of Sharing Uganda's Magic

Swiha Tours & Travel was founded in 2012 by a group of passionate Ugandan nature lovers, former park rangers, and hospitality professionals who shared one burning conviction: the world needed to know how extraordinary Uganda truly is.

Starting with just two vehicles and a handful of loyal clients, we have grown into one of Uganda's most respected tour operators — without ever losing the personal, family-like approach that defined us from day one.

Today, Swiha operates across Uganda, Rwanda, Kenya, and Tanzania, with a team of over 40 professionals dedicated to crafting journeys that go far beyond tourism — they create genuine connection.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🦁 Love for Wildlife

We are conservationists first and tour operators second. A portion of every booking supports wildlife protection and anti-poaching efforts.

🤝 Community First

We employ local guides, partner with community-owned lodges, and source food locally wherever possible.

✅ Absolute Honesty

What we quote is what you pay. No surprise fees, no misleading inclusions. Just honest, transparent service.

🌱 Eco-Responsibility

Low-impact travel isn't a trend for us — it's a founding principle. We operate carbon-offset vehicles and use eco-certified lodges.

🎓 Deep Expertise

Our guides hold formal qualifications in wildlife guiding, ornithology, and first aid. Knowledge you can trust in the field.

❤️ Genuine Hospitality

Ugandan warmth is legendary, and we make sure every traveller feels it — from airport arrival to farewell hug at departure.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a dream shared over campfire stories in Bwindi to one of Uganda's most trusted travel brands — this is the Swiha story.

2012
Swiha is Founded
Robert Ssebagala and two friends start Swiha with 2 Land Cruisers and a vision to share Uganda's wild beauty with the world.
2014
First International Clients
Our first bookings from the UK and Germany arrive, and Swiha earns its first TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence.
2016
UTB Certification & Fleet Expansion
Licensed by Uganda Tourism Board. Our fleet grows to 8 vehicles and we open our Kampala office on Kampala Road.
2018
Rwanda & Kenya Expansion
We extend our operations to Rwanda and Kenya, allowing us to offer seamless multi-country East Africa itineraries.
2020
Community Conservation Fund
Swiha launches the Swiha Community Conservation Fund, donating 2% of every booking to local conservation and education projects.
2024
2,000 Travellers Milestone
We celebrate our 2,000th happy traveller and are recognised as one of Uganda's Top 10 Tour Operators by the Uganda Tourism Board.

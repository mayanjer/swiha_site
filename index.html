Our Story

Born from a Love of Uganda's Wild Beauty

Founded in 2012, Swiha Tours & Travel was built on a simple belief: the best journeys are shaped by people who truly love where they're from. Our team of passionate Ugandan guides, naturalists, and hospitality professionals bring that love to every single trip.

We specialise in gorilla trekking, wildlife safaris, bird watching, and cultural immersion experiences that leave you with a deep connection to East Africa's most extraordinary destinations.
